Yakkee Exists to Erase Distance...

...between friends and family that aren't able to actually get together as much as they would like. Yakkee was designed to make it easy for people to spend time with friends and family residing in assisted-living facilities, hospitals and anywhere that risks leaving people feeling isolated and lonely.

thumb_up

So easy to use

The Yakkee interface was designed to be easy to use, paying special attention to the needs of people that may not be comfortable with technology or may those that have disabilities. Our interface allows the user to speak commands, use gestures or use traditional the keyboard and mouse.
